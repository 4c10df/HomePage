Finchglow Travels Nigeria Limited, a world-class Travel Management Company, officially opened to business on the 1st of June 2006. It offers Travel Management Services to all sectors of the economy, be it corporate or individual with special emphasis on corporate travels.

In this capacity, we manage the ravel needs of multinational, medium, and small companies depending on their travel budgets. The company maintains an annual sales turnover of over N25,000,000,000 (Twenty Five Billion Naira).

Travelden Nigeria Limited is a renowned online lifestyle travel Agency established in 2016. We provide affordable flight tickets, hotel booking and reservations, holiday and tour deals, visa assistance, insurance services, and more for our client’s utmost convenience.

In this capacity, we manage the travel needs of multinational, medium, and small companies depending on their travel budgets. The company maintains an annual sales turnover of over N4,000,000,000 (Four Billion Naira Only).

With its professional approach, experienced team, and excellent supplier relationships, FCM Nigeria uses its expertise to personalize travel management solutions to the unique needs of corporate customers.

LAGOS AVIATION ACADEMY (LAA) is an aviation training academy situated in the central district of Lagos Mainland. The Academy provides excellent training services enabling the beneficiaries to attain and maintain success in their desired careers. The key objective of the academy is to raise and support competent aviation professionals for successful careers in the industry.
