Finchglow Holdings is one of the leading Travel Management Companies in Nigeria. It is the parent company and holding group that serves as an umbrella for 6 other companies in the downstream sector of the Nigerian aviation industry.

The brands under Finchglow Holdings are Finchglow Travels, Finchglow Holidays, Travelden, FCM Travel Solutions, and Lagos Aviation Academy. Finchglow Holdings has office presence across Lagos, Abuja, and Portharcourt.

Activities of the group are:
Ticketing and reservations
Hotels
Protocols
Travel Management
Aviation Training
Asset Management

Finchglow Holdings is based in Nigeria with 8 offices across Lagos, Abuja and Port Harcourt. Our company, which has been in operation for 13 years, is regarded as the number one travel solutions organization in Nigeria owing to our consistency and delivery of quality and unequaled customer service.
